Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of whole wheat bread
 - 2 tablespoons of creamy peanut butter
 - 1 tablespoon grape jelly

Directions:
 1. Toast slices of bread
 2. Slather peanut butter on one slice of toast
 3. Slather jelly on the other slice of toast
 4. Combine pieces of toast with peanut butter and jelly facing each other
 5. Enjoy!
